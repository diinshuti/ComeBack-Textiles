Our Process

We collect textiles from different customers.
The donated clothes are then cleaned and prepared in order to be used for designing quilt blankets.
The clothes are then folded and kept neatly, ready for the designers.
The different fabrics are then cut into piece
The pieces are then put together to make a quilted blanket.
